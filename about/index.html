About.

Hi, I'm Josh.

I'm a serial volunteer and entreprenuer completing a Double Bachelor's Degree in IT and Multimedia. I have always been heavily involved in extra-curricular activities, including being a Peer Assisted Study Leader, Peer Mentor and a committee member at the ACS Gold Coast Chapter.

I have most recently worked in user experience design and strategy for the Commonwealth Bank.
Prior to that I worked as an IT manager and system and network administrator for a large non-profit whilst gaining a Certificate IV in IT. I was later awarded a TAFE Student Achievement Award.

I'm a natural leader and proven mentor, evidenced by being a Deputy Captain in the NSW Rural Fire Service

Other buzz words that describe me include technology enthusiast, first adopter, customer advocate and critical thinker.
I am a breaker of code. And then a fixer of code.
I have a huge passion for disruption using the web and mobile platforms. I am excited for what's to come.
