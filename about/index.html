About.

Hi, I'm Josh.
I'm a Telstra Tech Expert, startup cofounder and serial volunteer in my final year of university.

Whilst completing my Double Bachelor's Degree in IT and Multimedia, I have become heavily involved in university life, including being a Peer Assisted Study Leader, Peer Mentor and volunteer at various univeristy events. I am also a vlunteer at a number of pop culture expos and make it my mission to attend numerous tech related events such as Startup Weekend, hackathons, talks and YITCON. I am also currently a committee member at the ACS Gold Coast Chapter.

I have most recently worked in user experience design and strategy for the Commonwealth Bank. It was here I took on a large role for a key internal project, focusing on the user experience and user interface design.

Prior to that I worked as a system administrator, network administrator and tech support officer for a large non-profit. Whilst gaining my TAFE Certificate in IT, I was asked to manage the department whilst a review was carried out. I was later awarded a TAFE Student Achievement Award.

I'm a natural leader, a proven mentor and a highly observant person. One of my greatest passions is learning new things and passing on my knowledge to others. This was brought out, amongst other things, with my involvement in the NSW Rural Fire Service, where I remain a member. I was elected as a Deputy Captain by my peers, recognising my skills and knowledge in this area.

Other buzz words that describe me include technology enthusiast, first adopter, customer advocate and critical thinker.
I am a breaker of code. And then a fixer of code.
I have a huge passion for disruption using the web and mobile platforms. I am excited for what's to come.
